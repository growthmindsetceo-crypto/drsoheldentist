Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our dental services

Is the root canal treatment painful?

Not at all! With modern anesthesia and advanced techniques, root canal treatment at our clinic is completely painless. Most patients report feeling comfortable during the procedure and experience significant relief from toothache afterward.

How much do dental implants cost?

Dental implant costs vary depending on the number of implants and additional procedures needed. We offer competitive pricing in Berhampore and provide detailed cost estimates after your initial consultation. EMI options are available.

Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Our staff will help you understand your coverage and maximize your benefits. We also provide cashless treatment for approved insurance providers.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

For dental emergencies like severe toothache, broken teeth, or bleeding, call us immediately at 087787 15877. We offer 24/7 emergency services and will provide immediate guidance and pain relief.

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting every 6 months for routine checkups and cleanings. However, if you have ongoing dental issues or are undergoing treatment, we may suggest more frequent visits.
